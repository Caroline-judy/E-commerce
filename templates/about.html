Our Story

Sakhi is a boutique-style fashion brand that celebrates timeless elegance with a modern touch. Founded by Shyni, our collection reflects a passion for craftsmanship, sustainability, and empowering women.

We offer a curated range of traditional sarees, stylish kurtis, and unique jewellery designed to make every woman feel confident and beautiful.

Our Mission

To empower women through fashion that speaks to their individuality, with a commitment to sustainability and style.

Our Values

We believe in quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Our ethical practices and commitment to sustainability guide everything we do.

Our Vision

To expand globally while celebrating our rich heritage and introducing more sustainable fashion options for all occasions.
